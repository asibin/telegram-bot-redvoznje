*Search for bus, night bus, tram, trolley or minibus information*
To search for bus, night bus, minibus, trolley or tram schedules write /bus followed with line number you are searching for:

/bus 53

to get bus schedule for line 53.

for trains starting from _Batajnica_ or _Pancevacki_ most station
